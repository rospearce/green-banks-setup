Case making

Phase 1

The casemaking phase is about the reasoning/ justification for a different policy or instrument. It provides direction of what the future institution or reform could deliver if implemented. The proposal needs to demonstrate that it is aligned with the strategic goal of the country.

The key decisions and choices of this phase will be determined by a number of elements, from stakeholder engagement, decision making process, analytical support and standards and framework. Their interlinkages shapes the outputs of the phase which are the foundations for the next phase (click on each bubble to see more).
